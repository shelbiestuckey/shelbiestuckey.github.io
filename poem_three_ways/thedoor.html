OUT OF IT STEPS OUR FUTURE,THROUGH

THIS DOOR

ENIGMAS,EXECUTIONERS AND RULES,

HER MAJESTY IN BAD TEMPER OR

A RED-NOSED FOOL WHO MAKES A

FOOL OF FOOLS.

GREAT PERSONS EYE IT IN THE

TWILIGHT

FOR A PAST IT MIGHT SO CARELESSLY

LET IN,

A WIDOW WITH A MISSIONARY GRIN,

THE FOAMING INUNDATION AT A ROAR.

WE PILE OUR ALL AGAINST IT

WHEN AFRAID,

AND BEAT UPON ITS PANELS WHEN

WE DIE:

BY HAPPENING TO BE OPEN ONCE,

IT MADE

ENORMOUS ALICE SEE A

WONDERLAND

THAT WAITED FOR HER IN THE

SUNSHINE AND,

SIMPLY BY BEING TINY, MADE HER

CRY.

I. THE DOOR

W.H. AUDEN
